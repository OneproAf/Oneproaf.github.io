1. Introduction

Welcome to MoodScan AI ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mood analysis application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address, password (encrypted), and authentication data when you create an account
  • Profile Information: Any additional information you choose to provide in your user profile
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our Service, including mood scan frequency and preferences

2.2 Mood Analysis Data

  • Mood Results: Emotional analysis results including dominant emotions, confidence scores, and emotional percentages
  • Scan Timestamps: Date and time of each mood scan
  • Mood History: Historical mood data for trend analysis and personal insights

2.3 Image Processing

IMPORTANT: When you use our mood scanning feature, your image is processed as follows:
  • Images are sent to our secure backend server
  • Images are immediately forwarded to Google's Gemini API for analysis
  • We do NOT store, save, or retain your images after analysis
  • Images are processed in-memory and discarded immediately
  • Only the emotional analysis results are stored, not the original images

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Provision: To provide, maintain, and improve our mood analysis service
  • Personalization: To personalize your experience and provide relevant music recommendations
  • Analytics: To analyze usage patterns and improve our Service (using anonymized data)
  • Communication: To send you important updates about our Service
  • Support: To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Data Storage

  • All data is stored securely using Firebase Firestore
  • Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication
  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • We implement industry-standard security measures

4.2 Data Retention

  • Mood data is retained for as long as your account is active
  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Deactivated accounts are permanently deleted after 30 days

5. Third-Party Services

Our Service integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: Authentication and database services
  • Google Gemini API: AI-powered mood analysis
  • Spotify API: Music recommendations
  • Render: Backend hosting services
  • Netlify: Frontend hosting services

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing their policies for complete information.

6.1 Access and Control

  • Access your personal data and mood history
  • Update or correct your account information
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format

6.2 Opt-Out Options

  • Unsubscribe from email communications
  • Disable notifications in your account settings
  • Limit data collection through browser settings
